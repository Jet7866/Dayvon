How to Stay Focused & Manage Stress

Finals week is a test of endurance, organization, and mental resilience. The key to surviving is a structured study plan—break subjects into manageable chunks and use active recall techniques. Prioritize sleep and avoid all-nighters, as a well-rested brain retains information better. Hydration and balanced meals are crucial; excessive caffeine can cause burnout. Take short breaks using the Pomodoro technique to maintain focus and prevent exhaustion. Study groups can be helpful, but ensure they stay productive. Lastly, managing stress with light exercise or mindfulness techniques will improve retention and overall performance. Stay positive, believe in yourself, and remember—finals are just one step in your academic journey.
